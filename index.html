Introduction

This site links researchers with the various codesets developed by the PEDSnet team for identifying patient cohorts, treatments or outcomes in our database. We are making them available here for both potential collaborators and outside researchers whose work can benefit from what we have already done.

The codesets are based on the Common Data Model as described here . The exception is the “Query Logic” group which contains R files that draw on the codesets and implement other logic and calculations.

Usually codesets will be of the “ iccc ” form, integer concept_id and three text fields: concept_code , concept_name , and vocabulary_id . Note that for labs done, the performance of the lab may be listed as a Procedure, but the results of the lab may be listed as a Measurement.

Codeset Groupings

GroupBrief DescriptionVocabularies Typically Used
Conditions Diagnoses of interest in thecondition_occurrencetable SNOMED for searching on condition_concept_id , ICD 9 and ICD10 for searching on condition_source_concept_id . The Nebraska Lexicon is not used.
Drugs Diagnoses of interest in thedrug_exposuretable RxNorm for searching on drug_concept_id or, if necessary, on drug_source_concept_id
Procedures, including labs performed Specific procedures, including diagnostic tests, performed on a patient as described in the procedure_occurrence table CPT4, HCPCS, ICD9Proc, ICD10Proc
Measurements, including lab results Specific measurements of interest as described in the measurements table. Querying measurments should also check units. CPT4, HCPCS, ICD9Proc, ICD10Proc
Query code R or SQL code developed and tested to extract specific estimates (such as Z-scores or estimated Glomerular Flow Rate) or phenotypes (such as Glomerular Disease) Not applicable
